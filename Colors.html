About

The color wheel is a tool used to showcase the many different known colors and how they relate to each other. It showcases the hues and mixes of the different colors.

Hover over the color circles below to see which colors they are opposite of on the color wheel. These colors go well together!

It is estimated that there are more than 10 million distinct colors based on what we know about color today. Many websites use the HEX tool to allow choice between colors of different shades, this tool allows for 'only' 256 colors.
